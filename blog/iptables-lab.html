iptables Firewall Lab — Packet Filtering Basics

Lab write-up

Configuring Linux firewall rules with iptables involves working with three main chains: INPUT, OUTPUT, and FORWARD. In this lab I:

  • Allowed specific traffic based on ports and protocols
  • Blocked unwanted traffic
  • Filtered packets at the network layer

This taught me packet filtering fundamentals, firewall architecture, and how rules affect traffic flow. It's core cybersecurity engineering.
